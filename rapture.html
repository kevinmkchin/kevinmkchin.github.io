Rapture (Minecraft Clone)

Originally planned as a 3D adaptation of the rogue-like "Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead", Rapture is an unfinished demo of my Minecraft/Voxel engine written in Java and OpenGL. The project is augmented on my 3D game engine I wrote previously: Ajax-Engine.


This engine has implemented Chunk-loading, perfect Axis-Aligned Bounding Boxes, and Procedural World Generation.


[W][A][S][D] to move, [SPACE] to jump.
